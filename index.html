About Me

I'm Katie, When I was about 8 years old I decided I was going to be a hairstylist. By the time I was 12, I was cutting the hair of everyone in my family. While I've been behind the chair for several years now, I never want to stop learning and growing.


I'm a Master Designer at Salon by InStyle inside JcPenney. I work with a variety of hair types. I specialize in Highlights and Balayage.
